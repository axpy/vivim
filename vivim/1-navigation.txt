*Navigation*

h - left
j - down
k - up
l - right

Ctrl + f - page down
Ctrl + b - page up

w - go by next word (incuding any punctuation signs)
b - go by previous word (including any punctuation signs)
W - go by next word (only by spaces)
B - go by previous word (only by spaces)

1gg or gg - go to the top of file (1st line)
$G or G - go to the last line

10gg, ngg or 10G, nG - go to n line
