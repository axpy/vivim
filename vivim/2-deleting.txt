*Deleting*

x - delete/cut single character under cursor
x - delete and X - backspace
u - undo
Ctrl + r - redo

d - delete operator, combine it with motion, e.g
dw - delete next word
db - delete prev word
dl - delete next char (x - is an alias for dl)

3dw - three times delete next word (same as d3w which is delete three next words)
d30 - delete up to 30th line (including)

dd - delete/cut line
p - paste

A - go to the end of line and turn insert mode
a - move cursor right and turn insert mode
E - go to the end of word (separated by space)
e - go to the end of word (separated by any specific characeter)

. - repeat previous command
