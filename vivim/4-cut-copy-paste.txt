*Cut copy paste*

d - cut/delete
y - yank/copy
p - put/paste
cut-copy-paste = delete-yank-put

dd - delete line with \n
D - delete to the end of line from cursor without \n
yy - copy line with \n
Y - copy line without \n

:reg - list of all registers
:reg n nm - get specific registers
"" - unnamed register
"0 - "1, "a - "z  - named registers

"0 - yank register
"1 - "9 - delete registers, they will stack, "1 - the last deleted item

"ayy - copy line in "a register
2"bdw - delete 2 words into register "b
"Ayw - append a word to register "a

"_dw - delete a word into blackhole (without storing in any register)

p - put after cursor
P - put into cursor

ddp - move line below
xp - move character to the right
